The Coven of the Scaled Claw
The The Coven of the Scaled Claw is a cult of evil druids committed to resurrecting the evil black dragon Grimfang, also known as The Shadow. They are searching for the Staff of Valthrax in order to perform a ritual to resurrect Grimfang. They have kidnapped Alistair Wolfsbane in an attempt to lure Pine Remaka out so they can obtain the staff. They're currently holding Alistair Wolfsbane in the High Forest in some ruins. Thery're also harrassing the nearby village Thornwatch, which will come into play as well. They're abducting villagers from Thornwatch in during the night in an attempt to do a mass sacrifice to Grimfang.
